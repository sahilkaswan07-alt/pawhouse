📏 Monthly Body Condition Score
Answer these 5 quick questions once a month to check Bruno's body condition using the vet-standard 9-point BCS scale — the same simple checks a vet uses at a routine exam.
1. Can you easily feel the ribs without pressing hard?
2. Looking from above, is there a visible waist behind the ribs?
3. Looking from the side, is there an abdominal tuck (belly tucked upward)?
4. Are the spine, hips, and pelvic bones visible or easily felt?
5. Is there obvious fat build-up over the neck, chest, base of the tail, or belly?
